Ponderosa

This a an amazing buffet with the world's best chicken wings and steak. They also made butter rolls better than Texas Roadhouse. There was enough variety for anyone to enjoy. I as a kid would often eat mac n cheese and ice cream. Now they're closed which I truly miss.

Top 5 foods from Ponderosa

  1. Party Wings
  2. Mac n Cheese
  3. Dinner Rolls
  4. Steak
  5. Grilled Shrimp
